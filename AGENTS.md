# Law Firm Digital Twin Agent Instructions

## Scope

This repository is the implementation home for Law Firm Digital Twin, a green-data, synthetic/public-data-first simulation of an AI-first insurance defense law firm.

## Required Behavior

1. Treat tracked files as public-or-may-be-public.
2. Keep real client data, secrets, private source rows, raw emails, PACER purchases, and case-bound work product out of the repository.
3. Use `private/` only for ignored local working notes; it is not a secret manager.
4. Do not copy code, data, prompts, carrier guidelines, filings, or templates from other repositories unless a named release receipt exists.
5. Use real effective-dated public rules only through source-admission receipts; use synthetic tribunal, party, witness, expert, and case identities.
6. Preserve the world-first model: firms are players; only the kernel owns world consequences.
7. Enforce knowledge and authority structurally. Prompt-only enforcement is prohibited.
8. Keep AI/provider capabilities behind provider-neutral contracts and cassettes.
9. Never represent simulated outputs as legal compliance, production readiness, or real-world predictions.
10. Stop before external service access, publication, live legal data ingestion, or G3+ fidelity expansion unless Lowell explicitly authorizes that next boundary.

## Current Human Decisions

- H-1: first jurisdiction selection is delegated through a data-first rubric.
- H-2: priority local assets may enter reuse review, but no copying until per-asset release receipts are approved.
- H-3: first build scope is G0-G2 through the walking-skeleton acceptance test.
- H-5: use three-layer synthetic marking before native export: corpus-external provenance manifest, embedded `SYNTHETIC — LFDT` metadata, and visible watermark at export/sharing.
- H-6: multi-case capacity remains zero until weekly human-review capacity and sampling allocations are measured.
- H-7: `design-c-baseline-v1` is adopted and frozen; build depth-first and budget new obligations.
- H-10: a non-synced runtime root is selected only in local configuration; its machine-specific path must never be tracked. Canonical activation remains blocked until physical worker isolation and a separate backup location are qualified.
- H-11: multi-case and unattended spend remain zero until a measured cost ceiling is approved.
