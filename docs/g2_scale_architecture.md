# G2 Scaling Architecture

Status: implemented contract layer and static campaign specification as of 2026-07-18. It expands the stable employment-defense walking skeleton without authorizing another case family, G3 fidelity, public-data ingestion, an external model, or unattended execution.

## Architectural rule

The sealed world is the only owner of facts and consequences. Specialists can propose plans, records, renderings, or findings. They cannot mutate canonical truth, see oracle labels through operating prompts, self-approve, or promote themselves. The kernel admits accepted proposals; the evaluation plane receives sealed labels through a separate projection.

This separates four things that must not be collapsed:

1. a case-family manifest says what a family requires;
2. a sealed population blueprint says what kind of case to design;
3. the world kernel owns the resulting facts, chronology, knowledge, authority, and consequences; and
4. renderers produce artifacts only from bounded projections of those admitted facts.

## Population design

The deterministic allocator supports hundreds or thousands of varied matters while preserving exact declared distributions. A 1,000-case batch currently allocates:

| Axis | Distribution |
|---|---|
| Merits posture | 30% defense favorable, 30% claimant favorable, 30% balanced, 10% ambiguous |
| Evidence shape | 15% defense directional, 15% claimant directional, 40% mixed/conflicting, 20% noisy, 10% sparse/corrupted |
| Procedural quality | 82% progresses on merits, 6% curable defect, 4% dismissal without prejudice, 3% dismissal with prejudice, 5% other technical disposition |
| Representation quality | 55% well represented, 25% uneven, 15% material error, 5% systemic failure |
| Resolution path | 28% settlement, 20% mediation, 18% dispositive motion, 17% trial, 7% trial plus appeal, 10% arbitration |

The labels are authorial design targets, not legal predictions. They are sealed from firm players. Operating views expose only commitments. Evaluation joins occur later in a treatment-blind control plane.

## Evidence capability matrix

The first active matrix has eight employment-defense cells: email, HR record, policy, payroll, calendar, litigation document, expert material, and causal noise. Every cell declares:

- allowed sources and fact domains;
- structure and native metadata obligations;
- chronology, version, custody, and assertion lineage;
- minimum substantive-density and non-placeholder checks;
- protected fixtures;
- prohibited shortcuts; and
- a domain-plausibility escalation capability.

Evidence is planned causally. Irrelevant e-discovery must have a reason to exist?normal business operations, duplication, supersession, retention, stale drafts, copying practices, system exports, or bounded corruption. It cannot be random filler pasted into a directory.

## Persona, relationship, and voice model

Persona depth is modeled as time-varying state rather than a personality-label-to-prose shortcut. The contract includes:

- background and lived experience;
- education and training without treating education as intelligence;
- role, seniority, workload, incentives, goals, fears, loyalties, and constraints;
- relationship history, power, trust, conflict, audience, and code-switching;
- attention and detail habits;
- memory encoding, retrieval, confidence, contamination, and revision;
- risk tolerance, directness, drafting and revision behavior;
- stress, fatigue, accessibility needs, channels and tools;
- bounded knowledge frontiers; and
- change over time after events.

MBTI may be stored only as fictional self-description if useful to a story; it is not a validated causal engine. ?Left-brain/right-brain,? class-determines-grammar, profession-determines-voice, education-determines-intelligence, and protected-attribute-determines-outcome shortcuts are prohibited.

A message or document is therefore a function of the world facts the author is allowed to know, the author?s state at that time, relationship and audience, organization/subculture, channel conventions, purpose, workload, stress, prior thread context, and revision history. Voice fidelity is evaluated across a corpus, not from one stylized sample.

## Learning loops

Learning means improving performance inside declared synthetic tasks. It never means inferring real legal base rates, giving legal advice, or claiming real-world causal effects.

| Loop | State | Techniques | Promotion boundary |
|---|---|---|---|
| Mock-trial design recovery | design_only | calibrated probabilistic classification, uncertainty sets | blinded protected holdout plus human promotion |
| Settlement timing and value | design_only | calibration, randomized simulation policy evaluation, uncertainty | finance invariants, protected holdout, human promotion |
| Evidence relevance and conflict | candidate_g2 | hard-negative ranking, active learning, uncertainty | never drop on score; human fixture review |
| Voice, document, representation | candidate_g2 | clustering/drift, active learning, dedup/data valuation | qualitative review and human renderer promotion |
| Billing reduction and appeal | design_only | calibration and randomized simulation policy evaluation | finance invariants and human promotion |
| Generator curriculum and coverage | candidate_g2 | curriculum/adversarial generation, active learning, data valuation | distribution preservation and human campaign revision |

Every loop groups splits by sealed world, case family, organization, persona namespace, and seed lineage. The default split is 60% train, 15% calibration, 15% test, and 10% protected holdout. A case, persona namespace, thread, attachment family, duplicate, or superseded version cannot cross splits. Test and holdout findings cannot feed the generator. Promotion is never automatic.

The technique registry also includes representation drift detection, conformal-style uncertainty sets, causal-noise hard negatives, and duplicate/fixture-value analysis. These are hypotheses to qualify against revision-bound synthetic fixtures, not claims that an embedding or metric is truth.

## Controlled long-run campaign

The manifest at `campaigns/g2-scale-v1/campaign.json` is a static, validated campaign specification. It contains goals, gold standards, dependencies, phases, waves, subwaves, ten atomic jobs, owner/checker separation, leases, heartbeats, retries, checkpoints, one-repair limits, path ownership, budgets, and mandatory stops.

It is intentionally `specification_only`. The local validator cannot authorize unattended execution even if someone fills receipt-looking strings into the manifest. A future controller and Cursor/runtime adapter must be separately implemented, dry-run qualified, independently reviewed, and explicitly authorized by Lowell. Until then, the correct behavior is to validate the packet and stop at the human baseline gate.

## Executable artifact specialist mesh

The active G2 compiler now produces one ArtifactProductionManifest and one
ArtifactMeshLocalReceipt for every artifact. Each manifest binds the exact
case-family and evidence-domain pack, plan and renderer projection, persona
snapshot and bounded view, assertion scope, knowledge and authority frontiers,
relationship scope, lineage, metadata, and native-format simulation target.

Employment litigation records route to the intake writer; policies to the
contract/policy specialist; expert records to the expert-witness builder; and
emails, HRIS, payroll, calendar, and nonresponsive records to the employment
business-record writer. Four independent checker contracts cover continuity
and custody, bounded domain plausibility, document fidelity, and voice/
representation. Design-only families, detached or cross-case manifests,
writer-as-checker assignments, overclaiming receipts, provider-specific core
contracts, and unqualified corpus inputs fail closed. The public
generated/g2-specialist-mesh-v1 fixture contains aggregate coverage only.
No runtime AI specialist execution or canonical admission is claimed.

## Implemented projection and staged-artifact pipeline

The next additive G2 implementation wave is now executable:

- `case_compiler.py` compiles an active employment-defense blueprint into kernel-only sealed state plus separate operating, renderer, and evaluation projections.
- `persona_state.py` creates deterministic, matter-bound, time-local persona snapshots and least-privilege renderer views with exact assertion-grant and knowledge-frontier enforcement; renderer views are recomputed from their trusted snapshot before use.
- `evidence_contracts.py` defines case-scoped assertions, registry-owned role/channel authority, recipient relationship policy, artifact plans, graph-validated version lineage, required metadata, staged content commitments, custody origins, and local shape-only receipts that explicitly do not certify fact authority, lineage, or admission. `case_compiler.py` separately issues a noncanonical full-case qualification receipt only after sealed fact authority, lineage, projections, staged bytes, and local receipts all validate together.
- The provider-free fixture renderer produces substantive G2 text records from exact allowlisted assertions. Staged bytes declare `text/plain` separately from `simulated_target_native_format` throughout the plan, projection, operating, and staged surfaces. It is a contract-qualification baseline, not a native-format or court-grade document generator.
- Conflict detection derives incompatible staged assertions without receiving the sealed design explanation.
- Ten deterministic isolated compilations currently produce 90 staged artifact candidates across all eight active employment evidence capabilities.
- Checked-in public fixture manifests exclude seeds, axis labels, design labels, facts, persona state, renderer projections, and evaluator joins.
- The original world, kernel, cassettes, routes, and Berean execution path remain unchanged.

The load-bearing decision and rollback record is in `docs/decisions/g2_projection_pipeline_decision.md`.

The static campaign now has ten jobs. J100/J110/J120 may build independent portable cores; J130 must prove they compose before J200 creates fixtures; J210 audits only after J200. This closes the previous possibility of individually passing but incompatible modules.

## Implemented persona-aware channel-composition wave

The parallel G2 channel renderer is implemented without replacing the proven
baseline renderer:

- `composition_contracts.py` derives a bounded writing disposition from the
  canonical renderer view and defines separate geometry for email, HRIS,
  policy, structured-table, calendar, litigation-intake, expert, and
  ordinary-course-noise records.
- `case_compiler.py` binds a pre-axis presentation-scope commitment derived
  without sealed merits, outcome, evidence-direction, or evaluator labels, and independently revalidates that derivation from the admitted source blueprint.
- `communication_culture.py` derives organization-culture and individual-style
  projections from that safe scope, synthetic identities, and versioned catalogs.
  It is presentation-only: no role, profession, education, class,
  background, training, goal, liability, outcome, or evaluator label is a
  The current identity policy keeps defense-firm organization and actor
  commitments stable across matters while treating insured and independent
  expert rosters as case-local. Stable actor identity controls voice; a separate
  safe per-matter presentation episode may vary only realized geometry. This
  allows one attorney to remain recognizable without forcing every document
  into an identical layout.
  style cause.
- `persona_channel_renderer.py` creates a replayable composition plan and a
  text candidate whose factual segments each bind to exactly one authorized
  assertion. Nonfactual prose comes only from a versioned bounded lexicon.
- Raw background, education/training, and goal text are not prose inputs.
  Organization culture and individual disposition can affect bounded ordering,
  headings, grouping, openings, connectives, and closings only; they cannot
  change facts, credibility, competence, liability, or outcomes.
- The public renderer and corpus path accept only a projection reselected by
  index from a fully revalidated compilation and qualification receipt.
  Detached projections are explicitly unqualified test inputs and cannot enter
  the public fixture.
- `corpus_auditor.py` fails exact-body clones, revision/parent-version
  continuity violations, parent-content commitment mismatches, and integrity
  concentration failures.
- `corpus_diversity_evaluator.py` accepts and revalidates only qualified case
  batches, then independently measures realized voice/geometry families,
  maximum family share, presentation fingerprints, trivial variants,
  near-templates, within-author continuity, organization coherence,
  cross-author distance, and coverage sufficiency. It rejects forged raw-row
  provenance, fails opener-only fake diversity, and keeps bodies/persona features

- `generated/g2-channel-v1` contains public-safe commitments and aggregate
  integrity/diversity metrics for a parallel 10-case/90-artifact render. It
  contains no bodies, actor identities, assertion values, persona state,
  sealed labels, or evaluator joins.

The pilot has 90 unique bodies and 90 unique candidates across all eight
active channels. Its integrity and diversity reports pass the declared G2
gates: each eligible channel has at least three effective presentation
families, no effective family exceeds the declared concentration ceiling, and
the fixture is not composed of opener-only variants. Passing these gates is
accompanied by persistent-author voice-concentration warnings and a litigation
near-template warning. They remain calibration targets and block deep-realism claims. Passing the gates is
not a claim of deep human realism, authentic demographic representation,
native-format fidelity, or production-grade evidence.
