# G2 Persona-Aware Channel Composition Decision

Status: selected for additive implementation on 2026-07-18.

## Decision

Add a provider-neutral composition layer between an existing
`RendererArtifactProjection` and the staged-artifact pipeline.

The composition layer receives only the bounded renderer projection. It may use
its verified writing controls, channel, recipients, time, and exact allowlisted
assertions. It may alter rhetorical organization, assertion order, emphasis,
brevity, hedging, correction signals, and channel geometry. It may not create,
remove, reconcile, or change facts; infer credibility, intelligence, competence,
liability, or outcome; inspect a sealed/evaluation object; or claim native-file
fidelity.

The first adapter is deterministic and produces `text/plain` G2 fixtures.
A separate corpus auditor measures exact duplicates, structural-template
concentration, channel geometry, voice separation/continuity, assertion-trace
coverage, namespace/lineage continuity, and receipt binding.

## Placement

```text
trusted compiler and full-case qualification
  -> one-artifact RendererArtifactProjection
  -> ChannelCompositionContract
  -> WritingDispositionProjection
  -> CompositionPlan
  -> deterministic TextCompositionCandidate with segment/assertion trace
  -> staged artifact and local shape receipt
  -> persona-channel render receipt
  -> corpus fidelity audit
```

The composer never receives `CaseCompilation`, `SealedCaseState`,
`EvaluationCaseProjection`, a blueprint, a world bundle, or an oracle label.

## Causal policy

Allowed influences:

- register, sentence-length band, directness, organization, and revision habit
  from the canonical renderer persona view;
- workload and stress only as bounded brevity or explicitness controls;
- memory mode only as a source-limitation or calibration control;
- recipient count and relationship grants only as an audience-mode control;
- channel and capability contracts as document geometry;
- deterministic variation only through an input-bound stream key.

Forbidden influences:

- MBTI or personality typing as causal truth;
- left/right-brain claims;
- protected attributes or proxies;
- education or class as intelligence, grammar, or credibility;
- profession as a complete voice generator;
- background/training text as dialect or error generation;
- merits posture, design labels, resolution target, evaluator data, or outcome;
- unsupported dates, people, events, reasons, legal conclusions, or expert facts.

Facts are composed, not invented.

## Authority and receipts

A composition candidate and persona-channel receipt are local structural
evidence only. They remain noncanonical and do not establish factual authority,
lineage admission, or legal/document fidelity.

Source factual authority remains in the existing full-case qualification
receipt. A rendered-corpus fixture is qualified only when every source
compilation and case receipt validate, every composition trace and local shape
receipt replay, and the corpus audit passes. No receipt is canonical admission.

## Channel contracts
The public composition and corpus-audit entry points must not accept a detached
renderer projection as a trust root. They require the full validated
compilation, its recomputed full-case qualification receipt, and a projection
index; the implementation reselects that exact projection from the qualified
compilation before rendering. The explicitly named unqualified renderer exists
only for local composition-contract tests. Its output cannot enter the corpus
auditor or public fixture path.


Eight active G2 channels receive versioned contracts:

- email;
- HRIS record;
- policy document;
- structured table;
- calendar;
- litigation intake record;
- expert work record; and
- ordinary-course nonresponsive record.

Every contract defines required sections and fields, permitted nonassertive
blocks, permitted style operations, prohibited moves, assertion placement, and
the statement that output is a synthetic G2 text fixture rather than a native
email, CSV, calendar item, HRIS export, expert report, court filing, or
production business record.

## Rollout

Wave 1 is additive:

1. build composition contracts, writing dispositions, deterministic plans,
   candidates, traces, validators, and hostile fixtures;
2. render a parallel ten-case/90-artifact fixture from already-qualified
   renderer projections;
3. run the corpus auditor and write only public-safe commitments and metrics
   under `generated/g2-channel-v1/`;
4. prove the existing compiler, world, cassettes, Berean path, and original
   fixture remain unchanged.

The base compiler renderer is not replaced in Wave 1. Promotion to a default
renderer or a free-form model adapter requires a separately reviewed
qualification, protected fixtures, paired evaluation, human promotion, and a
rollback to the deterministic adapter.

## Deterministic gates

- exact assertion IDs appear once in the segment trace;
- body reconstruction from ordered segments is byte-identical;
- factual segments are generated only by the reviewed assertion clause
  renderer;
- nonassertive segments come only from a versioned lexicon;
- a detached, forged persona view or assertion set cannot cross the
  full-case-qualified rendering boundary;
- channel required sections and fields are present;
- renderer/media claims remain `text/plain`;
- sealed/evaluator terms and values are absent;
- prohibited causal shortcuts are absent;
- same request replays to identical hashes;
- forbidden-context perturbations cannot change body prose;
- exact duplicate bodies across unrelated logical artifacts fail;
- structural concentration and near-template reuse are surfaced, not hidden;
- conflicting assertions remain independently detectable;
- public audit output contains no bodies, assertion values, actor identities,
  persona state, or evaluator joins.

## Invalidation and stops

Requalify on any change to persona/view schema, evidence capability contract,
channel contract, lexicon, composition planner, adapter, validator, assertion
trace, relationship policy, privacy/authority surface, source compilation, or
corpus-audit policy.

Stop immediately on oracle/evaluator leakage, assertion invention/mutation/
omission, forged persona acceptance, native-format overclaim, unsupported
factual prose, or a receipt that overstates authority. Pause scale-up on
repeated channel-geometry, template-collapse, representation, or continuity
failures.
