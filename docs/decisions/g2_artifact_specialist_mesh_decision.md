# G2 Artifact Specialist Mesh Decision

Status: implemented for the active employment-defense G2 walking skeleton.

## Decision

Every compiled artifact is structurally bound to a provider-neutral production manifest. The manifest owns the artifact's planner, evidence-family writer, deterministic renderer adapter, and four independent checker roles. It also binds the exact artifact plan, renderer projection, persona snapshot and view, assertion scope, knowledge and authority frontiers, relationship scope, lineage, metadata contract, evidence capability, and active domain pack.

The mesh is an executable contract surface, not a claim that a family of AI agents ran. In G2, all assignment records say `runtime_executed=false`. The existing deterministic renderer remains the only generation path. Local mesh receipts prove only that the bounded contracts and hashes fit together; they cannot claim factual authority, complete lineage validation, legal correctness, or canonical admission.

## Employment writer routing

- Litigation and referral records: bounded employment intake-record writer.
- Policies: contract and policy specialist with contract-lineage gates.
- Expert records: expert-witness builder with source-boundary and independence gates.
- Emails, HRIS records, payroll summaries, calendars, and nonresponsive records: bounded employment business-record writer.

All artifacts also receive the case-type evidence planner, renderer adapter, continuity/custody checker, bounded domain-plausibility checker, document-fidelity checker, and voice/representation checker. Writer and checker identities must be disjoint.

## Activation and privacy boundary

Only the active `employment_defense_g2` domain pack may produce manifests. Design-only case families cannot activate a mesh. Specialist inputs exclude sealed case state, evaluation projections, source blueprints, full case compilations, private source rows, and oracle/design labels. The checked-in fixture exposes aggregate counts and commitments only; it contains no document bodies, raw matter/actor/recipient identifiers, persona state, assertions, or sealed labels.

## Learning-loop boundary

Mesh results may later feed a separately governed learning loop, but no generator, checker, or runtime adapter may promote itself or tune against protected holdouts. Runtime/provider changes, contract changes, validator changes, privacy/authority changes, or domain-pack activation expire qualification and require deterministic reruns plus independent review.

## Deferred

G3+ fidelity, runtime LLM specialist execution, public or purchased data ingestion, additional case-family activation, native-file generation, and external-service access remain outside the current authority boundary.
