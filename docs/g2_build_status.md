# G0-G2 Build Status

As of 2026-07-18, the repository contains a stable-candidate low-resolution employment-defense world plus additive provider-neutral scaling, staged-artifact, artifact-specialist-mesh, persona-aware channel-composition, communication-culture, corpus-integrity, and corpus-diversity pipelines. Its last completed full-suite baseline has 153 passing deterministic tests; the later dossier wave has 19 passing focused tests, and the bounded SimPy/adoption wave has 23 passing focused tests plus 22 passing G2/walking-skeleton regressions. A later whole-suite attempt timed out, so no newer whole-suite pass is claimed. Every active artifact has a compiler-bound production manifest and a local contract-only mesh receipt; full case qualification revalidates the mesh. Public rendering and public evaluators revalidate complete case qualification. Culture/style selection uses a source-verified pre-axis presentation scope that excludes sealed merits, outcome, and evaluator labels. Persistent defense-firm identities remain stable across matters; insured/expert identities are case-local. It remains synthetic and deliberately stops before G3 fidelity, external data access, runtime LLM specialists, unattended execution, jurisdiction claims, or production use.

## Implemented architecture

- world.py creates the sealed world, synthetic personas, organizational roles, evidence artifacts, native-format metadata, custody origins, case-strength posture, and oracle commitment.
- g2.py is the world-first kernel. Kernel-owned verb policies enforce role, authority, and knowledge requirements; it also owns deadlines, evidence state, custody, deposition, expert inputs, outcomes, finance, closure, serialized attempt cassettes, and replay.
- berean.py is the independent evaluation-plane auditor. It derives conflicts from artifacts and checks custody, knowledge and authority boundaries, deadline verification, expert independence, voice separation, finance, closure, and world-owned outcomes.
- simulation.py retains the original migration skeleton but binds public entry points to the G2 engine.
- tests/test_g2_contracts.py enforces the structural claims below with hostile forgery, custody, tamper, replay, and evaluator-join probes.
- case_manifest.py adds typed case-family, evidence, persona, specialist, and sealed population contracts; only employment defense is active.
- validators.py independently rejects malformed registries, tampered distributions, label leakage, and unknown or design-only case-family populations.
- learning_loops.py defines bounded synthetic evaluation loops, grouped split isolation, protected holdouts, human promotion, rollback, and claim boundaries.
- campaigns/g2-scale-v1 is a validated static campaign specification; its local validator is structurally unable to authorize unattended execution. See g2_scale_architecture.md.

The system uses no LLM or hosted-provider dependency. SimPy 4.1.2 is the only installed third-party runtime and is limited to a qualified, noncanonical schedule-planning adapter checked against a pure integer reference scheduler. Rendering cassettes remain deterministic and provider-neutral. Later model adapters must remain outside the world kernel and must be qualified by artifact family.

## Persona and evidence depth

The first world includes eleven synthetic actors with separate role, training, work style, goals, memory behavior, and voice signatures, including different deadline-calculation and deadline-review actors. Variation is contextual and behavioral; education, class, profession, or popular personality labels do not mechanically determine intelligence or grammar.

The evidence inventory currently covers:

- termination notice;
- HR note;
- policy excerpt;
- manager email;
- HR email;
- payroll attendance summary;
- manager calendar export; and
- irrelevant benefits email.

The manager and HR messages create a protected design conflict about decision timing. The operating review sees only the artifacts. Berean independently derives the incompatible claim values in the evaluation plane.

## Persona-aware composition qualification
- The persona-aware renderer is additive and does not replace the compiler's qualified baseline renderer.
- Public rendering and corpus auditing require the full validated compilation, its recomputed qualification receipt, and an exact projection index; detached projections remain explicitly unqualified test inputs.
- Corpus lineage checks cover revision continuity, parent-version identity, and the parent content commitment.
- Every factual segment replays from one exact allowlisted assertion; nonfactual prose is selected from a versioned bounded lexicon.
- Background, training, active goals, MBTI, left/right-brain ideas, class, and education are barred from causing grammar, credibility, competence, liability, or outcome. A metamorphic regression proves raw background/training/goal text cannot alter rendered prose.
- Organization culture and individual style affect ordering, headings, field grouping, openings, connective language, and closings only. They cannot alter authorized assertion bytes, evidence lineage, credibility, competence, liability, or outcome.
- Integrity and diversity are separate reports. The diversity gate requires multiple realized presentation families, bounded concentration, and nontrivial geometry; underpowered groups fail advancement instead of returning a green pass.
- Passing these G2 gates is not evidence of authentic demographic representation, longitudinal human consistency, native-document fidelity, or production readiness.
