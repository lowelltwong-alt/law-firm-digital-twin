# G0-G2 Walking Skeleton Acceptance

One seed must produce paired traditional and AI-first runs that:

1. receive the same synthetic referral and incomplete initial record;
2. resolve a nontrivial conflict/authority issue through proper gates;
3. open the matter under synthetic guidelines and coverage posture;
4. derive and independently verify deadlines from the active rule pack;
5. preserve, collect, review, and produce a small inconsistent evidence set;
6. coordinate and take one modeled deposition with actor-limited knowledge;
7. retain one modeled expert whose opinion remains independent;
8. branch reproducibly to settlement, mediation impasse, dispositive result, or parametric trial/appeal;
9. record work, submit an invoice, receive and appeal a reduction, post AR, apply payments, and reconcile books;
10. close the matter with procedural, reporting, retention, conflict-history, and finance conditions;
11. replay from ledger and cassettes to identical canonical hashes;
12. demonstrate that no firm arm accessed the oracle or bypassed a human gate;
13. produce treatment-blind comparison output labeled synthetic and non-predictive.
