# Law Firm Digital Twin Constitution

Law Firm Digital Twin is a deterministic litigation-world simulator. The world is the product; firms are players.

## Non-Negotiables

- Green simulation only: public rights-cleared sources or wholly synthetic data.
- No real client data, credentials, private source rows, raw email, or case-bound work product.
- Real effective-dated rules may be used only through source-admission receipts.
- Tribunal, party, witness, expert, carrier, and case identities are synthetic.
- The kernel owns causation, deadlines, balances, authority, and outcomes.
- Players act only through the Party Interface.
- Knowledge frontiers and authority gates are structural, not prompt-only.
- LLMs may render or recommend; they do not decide world consequences.
- Simulated approvals are clearly labeled as simulated.
- Outputs are synthetic simulation evidence, not legal advice, production readiness, or prediction.

## G0-G2 Scope

The authorized first build covers constitution, deterministic kernel, and a complete low-resolution labor and employment defense walking skeleton. Its initial public, source-visible GitHub release was approved on 2026-07-18. G3+ fidelity lanes, external services, PACER purchases, Gmail setup, production use, and reuse ports still require separate approval.
